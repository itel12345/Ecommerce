Our Story

How a simple idea grew into a thriving business

Our Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2010 by two college friends, Premium Brand started as a small garage operation with a simple mission: to create high-quality products that make people's lives better. What began as a side project quickly grew into a full-fledged business as word spread about our exceptional craftsmanship and attention to detail.

2010

Founded in a small garage with just $5,000 in savings

2013

Opened our first retail location in downtown Seattle

2016

Launched our e-commerce platform, expanding nationwide

2020

Reached 1 million satisfied customers worldwide
